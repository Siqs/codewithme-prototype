What is CodeWithMe?

CodeWithMe was made by our team for the STEAM Carnival 2014's Hacker Preview Day AppChallenge.

The objective of the project is to be a social newtork used to make the learning of programming languages and concepts easier, by letting the user choose who will be their partner on their learning. You can chat with anyone, but we make it a lot easier to find the exact one who needs to learn something from you or can share a bit of knowledge with you. CodeWithMe is not only for the begginer programmer but to the experienced one who wants to share ideas and thoughts too.

The prototype came to make the idea of Pair Program with Me easier to use, by having its own Social Network.

CodeWithMe application runs on IBM's Bluemix, as the AppChallenge requires.
